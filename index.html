About Bizcelona

Bizcelona is a curated WhatsApp community for entrepreneurs, freelancers, and professionals in or connected to Barcelona.

We bring together thoughtful, talented people who want to collaborate, share ideas, and grow. Whether you're launching something new, seeking support, or simply building local connections—this space was made for you.

Our community is closed, carefully vetted, and focused on quality over quantity. Join us to surround yourself with energy, ideas, and trusted relationships.

Community Guidelines

Simple rules that keep our community thriving

Be respectful and professional

Keep conversations focused on business and collaboration

No spam, unsolicited DMs, or group links

Give more than you take

Treat all conversations as private and confidential

Ask an admin if you're ever unsure about posting
